Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how Aamoda Pickles collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Order Information: Details of the products you purchase and your order history.
  • Payment Information: While we use secure payment gateways like Razorpay, we may collect limited information related to your payment to process your orders. We do not store your complete payment details.
  • Website Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and browsing patterns (collected through cookies and similar technologies).

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, shipping updates, and customer service inquiries.
  • To personalize your experience on our website.
  • To send you promotional emails and updates about our products (only if you have opted in).
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information with the following third parties:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us with payment processing, shipping, website analytics, and email marketing. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect website usage data and improve your browsing experience. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use secure payment gateways to protect your payment information.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or delete your data. Please contact us if you have any requests related to your personal information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at aamodapickles@gmail.com or call us at 7731021234.
